Case Study 03 · Professional Services · Canada
Local-to-National SEO Expansion for a Canadian Consultancy
Meridian Advisory Group — Toronto, Canada
Meridian Advisory Group, a financial consultancy serving the Canadian market, engaged me to transition their SEO presence from local search dominance in Toronto into a nationally competitive organic strategy. The initial audit identified significant gaps in technical infrastructure, including poor page speed, missing hreflang considerations for bilingual content, and negligible E-E-A-T signals across service pages.

The strategy prioritised building topical authority in financial advisory content, publishing a series of long-form pillar pages supported by cluster articles targeting high-commercial-intent queries. Author credentialing, expert citation building, and structured FAQ schema were deployed to meet Google's elevated E-E-A-T requirements for YMYL categories.
